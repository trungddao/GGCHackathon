About

Welcome to the Georgia Gwinnett College Hackathon! Join us for an exciting 1-day coding marathon where students come together to innovate, collaborate, and create solutions to real-world problems to contribute to the community. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just getting started, our hackathon is the perfect place to unleash your creativity and make a difference. Get ready to code, connect, and change the world!

How To Hack?

Judging Criteria

  • • Hackathon: Collaborative event for individuals or teams
  • • Short duration: Typically lasting a day or two
  • • Focus: Creative projects in software development or technology
  • • Activities: Brainstorming, coding, and innovation
  • • Goal: Create prototypes or solutions within the event's timeframe
  • • Environment: Fast-paced, fostering creativity and problem-solving
  • • Themes: Often centered around specific challenges or industry topics
  • • Benefits: Opportunities for networking, skill-building, and innovation
  • • Teams up to three people (ideal team: a coder + a designer + a project manager)
  • • All projects must be made here (no previous projects)
  • • Go to mentors table for help if you are stuck
  • • Submit by 4pm (wait for instructions)
  • • Present project live to judges
  • • Join Discord for instructions/updates
  • • How complete is it?
  • • How impactful?
  • • How good looking?
  • • How well does it fit the challenge?
